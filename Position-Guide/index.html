Research Assistant (4550)

Hourly · $15 – $20/hr Entry Level
▼
Follows research plans in order to collect, record and manipulate research data. Maintains accurate and detailed research records.

Education & Experience

  • Education: High School diploma or GED
  • Experience: 0 – 1 year
  • Prior experience in: Research, data collection, data entry, record management

Supervision & Independence

  • Follows research plans under direct supervision of the PI
  • Works under close, day-to-day guidance
  • Does not supervise others

Job Responsibilities

  • Following research plans to collect, record, and manipulate research data
  • Maintaining accurate and detailed research records and files
  • Performing basic data analysis to produce reports
  • Documenting results and processes
  • May test software prototypes
  • May interact with research subjects to perform job functions
  • Performing related duties as assigned
55

Research Associate I (4551)

Hourly · $17 – $24/hr Early-Career
▼
Assists in the development and execution of research projects including experiment design, analysis of data collected and writing up experiment results.

Education & Experience

  • Education: Bachelor's Degree
  • Experience: 1 – 3 years
  • Prior experience in: Research, research lab, research methodology, data collection & analysis

Key Skills Required

  • Problem-solving and reasoning skills
  • Analytical skills
  • Proficiency with software packages for data manipulation

Job Responsibilities

  • Using a variety of software packages to input, manipulate, collect and record results or observations
  • Performing research experiments following experimental protocol and procedures
  • Researching and documenting information; compiling and maintaining accurate and detailed records
  • Summarizing statistical information for analysis, examination or presentation
  • Writing technical reports
  • May build and validate quantitative models
  • Identifying problems and obstacles to progress; developing strategies to solve problems
  • May program software in support of job functions
  • Attending project meetings
56

Research Associate II (4552)

Hourly · $20 – $27/hr Mid-Career
▼
Under general supervision, designs experiments and research protocols. Performs complex analysis to report results.

Education & Experience

  • Education: Bachelor's Degree
  • Experience: 3 – 5 years
  • Prior experience in: Data collection & analysis, research methodology, research lab, research

Key Skills Required

  • Oral and written communication skills
  • Analytical skills
  • Problem-solving and reasoning skills

Job Responsibilities

  • Designing experiments and research protocols with some supervision
  • Collecting, verifying and managing data
  • Performing data analysis to produce reports; documenting and processing results
  • Developing quantitative models
  • Contributing to overall project planning; acting as lead to coordinate portions of a large project or study
  • Ensuring project deadlines are met
  • Writing and editing recommendations and reports
  • Maintaining administrative records; tracking and adhering to established budgets
  • May program software in support of job functions
58

Research Associate III (4553)

Salaried · $45k – $63k/yr Senior / Lead
▼
Independently designs experiments and research protocols. Independently sets up and performs sound experimental procedures. May perform lab management functions.

Education & Experience

  • Education: Master's Degree
  • Experience: 1 – 3 years
  • Prior experience in: Research, research lab, research methodology, data collection & analysis

Key Skills Required

  • Project management skills
  • Problem-solving and reasoning skills
  • Analytical skills
  • Oral and written communication skills

Job Responsibilities

  • Independently designing experiments and research protocols
  • Independently setting up and performing sound experimental procedures
  • Suggesting improvements in research methodology
  • Collecting, verifying and managing data; developing data analysis methods and protocols
  • Performing complex and original data analysis to produce reports
  • May develop computational modeling methods
  • May manage IRB approval process and document IRB policies
  • May program software in support of job functions

Lab Management Functions (if applicable)

  • Adhering to established lab budget and providing input to budget planning
  • Supervising purchasing and planning capital expenditures
  • Establishing and enforcing safety policies and procedures
  • Ensuring regulatory requirements are adhered to
  • Establishing policies and supervising the maintenance of administrative records
  • Supervising lab employees including assignment of day-to-day duties
  • Assisting with hiring
