Research Assistant

Hourly · $15 – $20/hr Entry Level
▼

Typical Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a Bachelor's degree
  • 0 – 1 years of research experience
  • Familiarity with basic research tools and methods

Supervision & Independence

  • Works under close, day-to-day supervision of the PI
  • Follows detailed instructions for all tasks
  • Does not supervise others

Key Duties

  • Assisting with experimental design, data collection, and lab or computational work as directed
  • Maintaining organized records of research data, procedures, and results
  • Conducting literature reviews and summarizing findings
  • Preparing samples, materials, or datasets for analysis
  • Operating and maintaining research equipment and software tools
  • Assisting with preparation of reports, presentations, and manuscript drafts
  • Ensuring compliance with safety protocols and research ethics guidelines
55

Research Associate I

Hourly · $17 – $24/hr Early-Career
▼

Typical Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
  • 1 – 3 years of research experience
  • Proficiency with standard research tools and methods

Supervision & Independence

  • Works with regular PI guidance, with increasing independence
  • May provide guidance to student assistants
  • Participates in research group meetings

Key Duties

  • Designing and conducting experiments, simulations, or analyses
  • Collecting, processing, and analyzing research data
  • Contributing to development and refinement of research protocols
  • Preparing technical reports, visualizations, and documentation
  • Assisting with publications, conference papers, and grant proposals
  • Maintaining and troubleshooting research equipment and systems
  • Mentoring junior research staff or student assistants as needed
56

Research Associate II

Hourly · $20 – $27/hr Mid-Career
▼

Typical Qualifications

  • Master's degree or equivalent research experience
  • 3 – 5 years of demonstrated research experience
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Supervision & Independence

  • Works independently with minimal oversight
  • Supervises and mentors junior staff, RAs, and students
  • Collaborates with internal and external partners

Key Duties

  • Independently designing, conducting, and analyzing experiments or computational studies
  • Developing and optimizing research methodologies and analytical frameworks
  • Leading specific research tasks or sub-projects with associated timelines
  • Performing advanced data analysis, statistical modeling, or computational work
  • Co-authoring peer-reviewed publications, reports, and conference presentations
  • Writing sections of grant proposals and assisting with budget planning
  • Supervising junior research staff and student researchers
58

Research Associate III

Salaried · $45k – $63k/yr Senior / Lead
▼

Typical Qualifications

  • Master's degree or PhD preferred
  • 5+ years of extensive research experience
  • Strong publication record and proven leadership ability

Supervision & Independence

  • Self-directed; provides scientific and technical leadership
  • Mentors and supervises associates, assistants, and grad students
  • Represents the research group externally

Key Duties

  • Leading design, execution, and management of complex research projects
  • Developing novel research methodologies and analytical approaches
  • Serving as primary or co-author on peer-reviewed publications
  • Leading preparation of grant proposals, including budget development
  • Managing project timelines, deliverables, and institutional coordination
  • Representing the group in meetings with funding agencies and review panels
  • Contributing to strategic planning for the research group's direction
